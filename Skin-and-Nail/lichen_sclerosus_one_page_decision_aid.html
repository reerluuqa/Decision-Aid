Adjuncts Supportive Measures

  • Wash with bland emollients; avoid soaps/irritants and tight clothing.
  • Use lubricants for intercourse; provide reliable patient info/support resources.
  • Advise immediate review if persistent new lump, ulcer/erosion, or skin thickening.
  • Treat secondary infection when present.
  • Document treatment quantities and application technique; address steroid phobia and application difficulties (e.g., elderly).
