Vitamin D Preparations - Detailed ‚ñº

Calcipotriol: 50mcg/g, max 100g/week

Calcitriol: 3mcg/g, max 30g/day

Tacalcitol: 4mcg/g, max 10g/day

Contraindications: Calcium metabolism disorders, severe liver/kidney disease

Emollient Guidance ‚ñº

Quantity: 250-500g/week

Application: Apply liberally and frequently (every 2-3 hours)

Fire Risk: Paraffin-containing products are flammable - warn patients

Avoid: Aqueous cream as leave-on (high risk of reaction)
