Pregnancy & Breastfeeding Special Consideration

May use if benefits outweigh risks.

  • Breastfeeding: Use creams (easier to wash off)
  • Apply to nipple/areola: Use mild-moderate potency only
  • Wash off with warm water before each feed

Adverse Effects to Monitor
Local Effects
Skin atrophy, telangiectasia, acne, folliculitis, striae
Systemic Effects
Adrenal suppression, Cushing's, growth retardation
Ocular Effects
Cataract, glaucoma (with periorbital use)
Psychological
Rare: hyperactivity, sleep disorders, anxiety
Topical Steroid Withdrawal (TSW) Rare but Serious

Occurs after long-term/inappropriate use, especially moderate-high potency.

Red Flags for TSW:
‚Ä¢ Burning > itching as main symptom
‚Ä¢ Confluent redness beyond treatment area
‚Ä¢ Unusual distribution or appearance
‚Ä¢ Prolonged use history (>1 year, or >2 months in children)
