üìà Prognosis
  • 80-85% with diverticula remain asymptomatic
  • < 5% develop diverticulitis
  • Most uncomplicated cases recover with medical treatment
  • ~1/3 experience recurrence (50% within 1 year)
  • Complicated disease risk highest at first episode

‚úÖ Diverticulosis Management
Key Message

Asymptomatic - no specific treatment needed. Focus on prevention.

Lifestyle Advice

  • High-fibre diet (whole grains, fruit, vegetables)
  • No need to avoid seeds, nuts, or popcorn
  • Adequate fluid intake
  • Regular exercise
  • Weight management
  • Smoking cessation
