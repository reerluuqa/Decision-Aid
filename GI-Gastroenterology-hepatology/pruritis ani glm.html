Categorized by underlying mechanism.

PRIMARY (IDIOPATHIC) Functional condition caused by faecal contamination, bacterial enzymes, and moisture. Flatulence also contributes.

Management Strategy

1
Hygiene Modification

Wash with plain water (no soap) after bowel movements and at bedtime. Use a shower jet or sitz bath.

2
Drying

Pat dry (do not rub). Use soft towel, cotton swabs, or hair dryer on cool setting.

3
Clothing & Environment

Wear loose cotton underwear. Avoid tight clothing. Keep area cool at night (light sheet). Avoid biological laundry detergents.

4
Diet & Bowel Habit

Ensure stools are formed and regular. Adequate fibre intake. Avoid triggers (coffee, spicy food, citrus, alcohol, beer).

5
Scratching Prevention

Keep nails short. Wear cotton gloves at night.

Symptomatic Treatment

Excoriated Skin?

Prescribe soothing cream/ointment containing Zinc Oxide. Some find Vaseline¬Æ before bowel motion helpful (barrier).

Inflamed Skin?

Prescribe Hydrocortisone 1% (cream or ointment). Apply thinly, max twice daily, for no longer than 7 days.

Sleep Disturbance (Nocturnal Itch)

Consider sedating antihistamine Chlorphenamine (off-label use).
