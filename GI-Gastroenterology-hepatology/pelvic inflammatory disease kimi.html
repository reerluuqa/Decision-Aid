üìÖ Follow-up

  • Review within 72 hours: check response, compliance, alternative diagnosis
  • 2-4 weeks post-treatment: verify completion, partner treatment, contraception
  • Test of cure:
    • Gonorrhoea: 2-4 weeks
    • Chlamydia: 3-5 weeks (if indicated)
    • M. genitalium: 4 weeks

üë• Sexual Partner Management

All current & recent partners (6 months) need screening & treatment.

  • Refer to sexual health service for STI screen & contact tracing
  • If unable to attend: offer screening + empirical doxycycline 100mg BD for 1 week
  • Abstain from sex until both completed treatment & test of cure
  • Advise barrier method for future protection

üìã Key Prescribing Notes

Ceftriaxone

Contra: Cephalosporin/penicillin allergy

Caution: Colitis, renal/hepatic impairment

Doxycycline

Contra: Pregnancy, breastfeeding, porphyria

Caution: Photosensitivity, hepatic impairment

Metronidazole

Contra: Cockayne syndrome

Caution: Hepatic impairment, avoid alcohol

Fluoroquinolones

MHRA Warning: Only when other antibiotics inappropriate. Risk of disabling/irreversible side effects (tendon, neuropathy, CNS). Stop at first sign of serious reaction.
