Bacterial Vaginosis Clinical Algorithm

A concise reference guide for diagnosis and management in primary care

Last updated: July 2023

Definition & Symptoms

Vaginal dysbiosis characterized by:

  • Overgrowth of anaerobes (Gardnerella, Prevotella)
  • Loss of lactobacilli
  • Vaginal pH >4.5

Symptoms:

  • Thin, grey/white homogeneous discharge
  • Fishy odor (especially after intercourse)
  • No itching or soreness (suggests alternative diagnosis)
  • ~50% of women are asymptomatic

Risk Factors

  • Sexual activity (especially with multiple partners)
  • Recent change in sexual partner
  • Vaginal douching
  • Cigarette smoking
  • Copper intrauterine device (IUD)
  • Not using condoms
  • Ethnicity: Higher prevalence in Black women (45-55%) vs Caucasian (5-15%)

Protective factors:

  • Circumcised partner
  • Consistent condom use
  • Hormonal contraception

Differential Diagnosis

  • Candidiasis: White, odourless, curdy discharge + itching
  • Trichomoniasis: Yellow/green frothy discharge + itching/soreness
  • Chlamydia: Often asymptomatic, purulent discharge, no itch
  • Gonorrhoea: Purulent discharge, pain, no itch
  • Genital herpes: Ulceration, pain, watery discharge
  • Physiological discharge: No irritation, varies with cycle
  • Atrophic vaginitis: Postmenopausal women
  • Foreign body: Retained tampon

Recurrent BV Management

Recurrence rates:

  • 23% at 1 month
  • 43% at 3 months
  • 58% at 12 months

Management:

  • Repeat treatment with 7-day course of oral metronidazole
  • For frequent recurrences: Consider suppressive therapy with twice-weekly intravaginal metronidazole gel 0.75% for up to 6 months
  • Refer to specialist if recurrences continue

Complications & Counseling

Complications:

  • Increased risk of STIs (2-9x higher)
  • Pelvic inflammatory disease (PID)
  • Obstetric complications (preterm delivery, miscarriage)
  • Postpartum endometritis
  • Post-surgical infections

Counseling:

  • Avoid douching and vaginal irritants
  • Consider condom use
  • Smoking cessation
  • STI screening for high-risk patients
