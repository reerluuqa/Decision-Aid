Blood Cultures

Sepsis, Endocarditis, Bacteraemia

Collection: BD BACTEC bottles (Aerobic Silver/Blue, Anaerobic Purple)
Volume: Adults: 8-10mL per bottle | Children: 1-3mL
Timing: Before antibiotics if possible. 2 sets from 2 sites within 24hr
Transport: DO NOT REFRIGERATE Incubate within 4 hours
Turnaround: Prelim: 24hrs Final: 6 days

CSF (Cerebrospinal Fluid)

Meningitis, Encephalitis

Container: CE marked leak-proof container
Volume: Minimum 1mL for routine | 6mL for TB
Precautions: DO NOT POD Send 3rd/last tube
Turnaround: Microscopy: 30-60min

CMV PCR (Viral Load)

Cytomegalovirus Quantitation

Specimen: EDTA blood, urine, CSF
Volume: Minimum 500µL
Storage: Ambient or refrigerated
TAT: 3 working days
