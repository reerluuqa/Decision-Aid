Prevention & Patient Advice

Transmission Prevention

  • Avoid kissing/oral sex until healed
  • Don't share makeup, lip balms, utensils
  • Wash hands after touching lesions
  • Defer dental treatment until healed
  • No school exclusion for well children

Recurrence Prevention

  • Use SPF 15+ sunscreen/sunblock lip balm
  • Avoid known triggers (stress, fatigue, UV)
  • Antiviral prophylaxis not routine
  • Consider specialist referral for frequent/severe cases
