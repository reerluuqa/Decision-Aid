Empirical Antibiotic Guidelines

First Line (No penicillin allergy)

Piperacillin-tazobactam 4.5g IV 8-hourly
PLUS Gentamicin 5mg/kg IV daily (if severe/critical)

Penicillin Allergy

Meropenem 1g IV 8-hourly
OR Aztreonam 2g IV 8-hourly

Add Vancomycin if:

  • Central line present
  • Skin/soft tissue infection
  • Known MRSA colonization
  • Severe sepsis/shock

Investigations

  • FBC, U&E, LFT, CRP
  • Blood cultures x2 (aerobic & anaerobic)
  • Urinalysis & culture
  • Chest X-ray
  • Consider viral PCR, fungal markers

Fluid Resuscitation

  • 500ml bolus if hypotensive
  • Reassess after each bolus
  • Aim MAP >65mmHg
  • Consider inotropes if refractory
