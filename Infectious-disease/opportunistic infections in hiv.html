Treatment Protocols
‚ñº
1. Tuberculosis Treatment
  • Standard 4-drug regimen (HRZE) for 2 months
  • Continuation phase (HR) for 4 months
  • Consider corticosteroids for CNS involvement
  • Drug interactions with ART must be managed
Start ART within 2 weeks (except CNS TB)
2. Specific OI Treatments
PCP

Trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole 21 days

Prednisone if PaO2 < 70 mmHg

Monitor oxygen saturation
Cryptococcal Meningitis

Amphotericin B + Flucytosine (2 weeks)

Fluconazole consolidation (8 weeks)

Daily lumbar puncture if ICP elevated
MAC

Clarithromycin + Ethambutol ¬± Rifabutin

Lifelong secondary prophylaxis

Can discontinue if CD4 > 100 cells/ŒºL for 6 months
