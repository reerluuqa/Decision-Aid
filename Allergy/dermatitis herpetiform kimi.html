Clinical Pearls & Differential Diagnosis
Key Clinical Features:
  • Extremely pruritic (universal)
  • Grouped vesicles on extensors
  • Symmetrical distribution
  • May have only excoriations
  • Oral aphthous ulcers (20%)
Rule Out:
  • Atopic dermatitis (no IgA)
  • Scabies (burrows, nocturnal)
  • Contact dermatitis (history)
  • Linear IgA disease (linear deposits)
  • Bullous pemphigoid (tense blisters)
