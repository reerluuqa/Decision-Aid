Lab Result Variation Guide

Determine if a change in lab values is clinically significant or due to biological/analytical variation. Based on BMJ 2020;368:m149.

How to use: Find the test below. If a patient's results change by at least the percentage shown, it is likely significant. Smaller changes are likely due to normal biological variation.
Enter two results for the same patient to see if the difference is clinically significant or likely due to normal variation.
