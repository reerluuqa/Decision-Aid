Haematospermia is the presence of blood in seminal fluid.

  • Bright red = recent bleeding
  • Brown/dark red = older blood
  • May be single or recurrent episode.

üìà Prognosis & Patient Advice

Most cases are benign and self-limiting.

Prognosis:

  • 89% resolve spontaneously (median duration: 1.5 months)
  • Recurrence rate: ~13.5%
  • Malignancy: <7% in men >40, but higher than general population

Patient Advice:

  • Fertility usually unaffected (except untreated chlamydia/gonorrhoea)
  • Provide patient resources:
    • NHS: Blood in semen
    • BAUS: Blood in the semen
