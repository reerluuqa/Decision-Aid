Treatment Options
CPG 1 Watchful Waiting / Active Surveillance ‚ñº

Watchful Waiting: Control not cure. Deferred hormone therapy if needed. Suitable for older men/significant comorbidities.

Active Surveillance: Avoid overtreatment. Repeat biopsy at intervals. Treatment offered only if risk increases.

CPG 2‚Äì5 Radical Treatments ‚ñº
  • Radical prostatectomy ‚Äì Open or laparoscopic/robotic
  • External beam radiotherapy (EBRT)
  • Brachytherapy ‚Äì Low or high dose rate

CPG 2‚Äì5: Offer radiotherapy + ADT in combination if non-surgical

Advanced Adjunctive / Palliative ‚ñº
  • Hormone therapy: LHRH agonists/antagonists, anti-androgens
  • Chemotherapy: Docetaxel, Cabazitaxel (hormone-relapsed)
  • Bisphosphonates: For osteoporosis on ADT; bone pain
