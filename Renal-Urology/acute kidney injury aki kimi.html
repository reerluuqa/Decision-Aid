Pre-renal Causes (70%)

  • Hypovolaemia (GI losses, bleeding, burns)
  • Hypotension (sepsis, cardiogenic shock)
  • Drugs: ACEi, ARB, NSAIDs, diuretics
  • Hepatorenal syndrome
