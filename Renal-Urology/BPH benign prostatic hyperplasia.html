üî¥ Urgent Referral Indications

  • Recurrent urinary retention
  • Renal insufficiency (elevated creatinine)
  • Hydronephrosis on imaging
  • Recurrent urinary tract infections (UTIs)
  • Gross recurrent hematuria

  • Abnormal DRE: nodules, asymmetry
  • Elevated PSA for age (see guidelines)
  • Microscopic hematuria
  • History of prostate cancer
  • Refractory to medical therapy

Definition

BPH involves hyperplasia of epithelial and stromal prostatic tissue, causing bladder outlet obstruction. Two components: STATIC (increased tissue narrowing urethra) and DYNAMIC (increased smooth muscle tone via alpha-adrenergic receptors).

üìà Epidemiology Key Points

  • Linear increase with age
  • Higher in Eastern Europe (6,480/100,000)
  • Lower in North Africa/Middle East (987/100,000)
  • Increasing global burden due to aging population

‚öóÔ∏è Pathophysiology

  • Increased stromal:epithelial ratio
  • Alpha-1A receptors predominant in prostate
  • Testosterone ‚Üí DHT conversion drives growth
  • Aging-related hormonal changes

Watchful Waiting: Reassurance, lifestyle modifications, annual review. Few progress to retention.

Moderate-Severe (IPSS 8-35)

Medical Therapy: Alpha-blocker first-line. Add 5-ARI if prostate >30g. Consider combination if progression risk.

First-line for moderate-severe symptoms

Fast onset (days), well-tolerated. Works by relaxing smooth muscle in prostate and bladder neck.

Black Box Warnings

Increased risk of falling, fractures, hypotension, and head trauma. May increase heart failure risk (especially non-selective).

For enlarged prostates (>30g)

Reduces prostate size, decreases risk of retention and surgery. Slow onset (months). Must be long-term.

Drug Dose Key Side Effects Special Notes
Finasteride 5 mg daily Sexual dysfunction (5-10%) Only beneficial if prostate >30g. Halves PSA values.
Dutasteride 0.5 mg daily Sexual dysfunction, gynecomastia Also halves PSA. Rare: depression, suicidal thoughts.

Tadalafil 5mg daily is FDA-approved for BPH. Improves LUTS and erectile function.

Drug Dose Key Side Effects Special Notes
Tadalafil 5 mg daily Headache, flushing, dyspepsia Only approved PDE-5 for BPH. Second-line option.

Improve overactive bladder symptoms. Previous concerns about worsening retention appear unfounded.

When to Consider Combination

Moderate-severe symptoms at risk of progression, or inadequate response to monotherapy.

Combination Indication Benefits
Alpha-blocker + 5-ARI Large prostate (>30g), progression risk >4 point IPSS improvement. Reduces retention/surgery risk.
Alpha-blocker + Anticholinergic Storage symptoms predominant Improved QoL, better storage symptom control.
Alpha-blocker + Beta-3 agonist Persistent storage symptoms Mirabegron 25-50mg daily - good alternative to anticholinergics.
5-ARI + Tadalafil Large prostate + ED FDA-approved combination (5mg/5mg).

Case 1: 60-year-old with frequency and nocturia

Presentation: 3-month history of urinary frequency without burning, nocturia 3x/evening.

Exam: Moderately enlarged prostate, no nodules.

Action: Behavioral modifications first. Consider alpha-blocker if bothersome.
