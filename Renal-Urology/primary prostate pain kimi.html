Initial Assessment Protocol

History Taking

  • Abdominal examination
  • External genitalia
  • Pelvic floor assessment
  • DRE with prostate palpation
3

Initial Tests

  • Urinalysis & culture
  • STI screening if indicated
  • NIH-CPSI score
  • UPOINT classification
4

Rule Out

  • Bacterial infection
  • Interstitial cystitis
