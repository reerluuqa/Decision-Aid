Definition

Adenocarcinoma of Unknown Primary Site (ACUP) ‚Äî Metastatic adenocarcinoma where the primary site is undetectable despite thorough physical exam, laboratory tests, and radiographic investigation.

Key Goal: Identify favourable subtypes (‚âà20%) for tailored therapy.
