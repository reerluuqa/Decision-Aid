Step 1

  • Examination: location (midline/anterior/posterior triangle), size (>3 cm suspicious), consistency (hard/irregular), mobility/fixation, tenderness, pulsatility, fluctuation, skin changes, moves on swallowing or tongue protrusion (thyroid/thyroglossal).
  • Head & neck + general: oropharynx, scalp/skin, ears/nose, chest, abdomen, axillae/groin; check hepatosplenomegaly; consider unilateral OME (possible nasopharyngeal malignancy).
