Safety Netting & Definition
Safety Netting Advice
  • Explain specific red flag symptoms to monitor.
  • False-negative chest X-rays can occur.
  • Review if symptoms persist, recur, or worsen.
  • Low risk doesn't mean No risk — reassure but monitor.
Definition: "Unexplained"

Symptoms or signs that have not led to a diagnosis being made by the healthcare professional in primary care after initial assessment (including history, examination, and any primary care investigations).
