Lifestyle Advice

  • Nutritional supplements: Folic acid 400mcg/day (pre-conception to 12 weeks), Vitamin D 10mcg/day throughout
  • Diet: Balanced diet, avoid unpasteurized, undercooked foods, pâté, liver
  • Exercise: Regular moderate exercise
  • Avoid: Alcohol, smoking, recreational drugs
  • Medications: Discuss safety of all medications
Folic Acid Vitamin D Avoid Alcohol No Smoking

Red Flags - Urgent Referral

  • Symptoms of pre-eclampsia (headache, visual disturbances, epigastric pain, swelling)
  • Reduced fetal movements
  • Vaginal bleeding
  • Severe hypertension (≥160/110 mmHg)
  • Ruptured membranes

Screening Tests

  • Infectious diseases: HIV, syphilis, hepatitis B
  • Blood disorders: Sickle cell and thalassaemia
  • Fetal anomalies: Via ultrasound scans
  • Gestational diabetes: If risk factors present
HIV Syphilis Hep B Diabetes

Risk Assessments

  • Pre-eclampsia: Consider aspirin 75-150mg/day from 12 weeks if high risk
  • Gestational diabetes: Screen if BMI >30, previous macrosomia, previous GDM, family history, high-risk ethnicity
  • VTE: Assess at booking and after hospital admissions
  • Fetal growth restriction: Consider if previous SGA, stillbirth, medical conditions
Pre-eclampsia Diabetes VTE

Mental Health & Wellbeing

  • Assess at every appointment
  • Ask about domestic abuse (private discussion)
  • Provide information on support resources
  • Discuss emotional changes during pregnancy
  • Screen for depression and anxiety
