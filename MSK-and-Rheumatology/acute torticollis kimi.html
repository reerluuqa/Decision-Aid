üíäPharmacological Treatment

First-line: Oral analgesics
  • Paracetamol: 1g every 4-6 hours (max 4g/day)
  • NSAIDs: Ibuprofen 400mg TDS or Naproxen 250mg BD
  • Consider PPI co-prescription for GI protection
Second-line: Combination therapy
  • Paracetamol + NSAID
  • Codeine: 30-60mg every 4-6 hours (max 240mg/day)
  • Consider opioid risks (sedation, constipation)

üèÉNon-Pharmacological Treatment

Heat/Cold therapy: Apply for 15-20 minutes, 3-4 times daily
Gentle exercises: Neck rotation and stretching exercises
Posture advice: Ergonomic workstation setup
Pillow advice: Low, firm pillow for sleeping

üìäExpected Course

Acute torticollis: Usually resolves within 24-48 hours, occasionally up to 7-10 days
Recurrence: Common, especially without preventive measures

üéØFollow-up Plan

Self-check at 48 hours: Review pain and mobility
  • If improving: Continue self-management
  • If not improving: Consider GP review
  • If worsening: Urgent medical review
GP review at 1 week: If symptoms persist
Physiotherapy: If symptoms persist > 2 weeks or recurrent episodes
