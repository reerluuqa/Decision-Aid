CRITICAL: Atypical Presentation

Sudden behavioral change often = PAIN or ILLNESS.

  • Action: Assume physical cause first. Do not attribute to "just their learning disability".
  • High Risk: Respiratory infections, constipation, dental pain, silent MI.
Immediate Check: Does the patient have a Hospital Passport? Read it immediately.

LD vs Learning Difficulty (Click to expand)
Learning Disability Global impairment (IQ < 70), affects understanding complex info & independent living.
Learning Difficulty Specific impairment (e.g., Dyslexia, ADHD, Dyspraxia). Global IQ usually normal.

Mental Capacity Act (5 Principles)

For people aged 16+. Assessment must be decision-specific.

Show/Hide MCA Principles
  1. Presume Capacity: Until proven otherwise.
  2. Maximize Support: Take all practicable steps to help them decide.
  3. Unwise Decisions: People have the right to make decisions others might think are unwise.
  4. Best Interests: Decisions made for them must be in their best interests.
  5. Least Restrictive: Consider if the outcome can be achieved in a less restrictive way.
