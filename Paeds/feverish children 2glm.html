Antipyretic Protocol

‚úÖ First-line Treatment

Paracetamol OR Ibuprofen - not both simultaneously

Use if child is uncomfortable/distressed, not just for fever

When to Switch/Alternate

  • If monotherapy ineffective after 1 hour
  • Consider alternating if distress persists
  • Paracetamol: every 4-6 hours
  • Ibuprofen: every 8 hours
  • Keep medication diary

Safety Netting

Return Immediately If:

  • Non-blanching rash develops
  • Child has a seizure
  • Signs of dehydration persist
  • Fever lasts >5 days
  • Child becoming more unwell
  • Parent unable to cope
