Treatment Priorities
  1. Medical stabilization - address red flags first
  2. Weight restoration - 0.5-1kg/week target
  3. Psychological therapy - CBT-ED, MANTRA, or SSCM
  4. Family involvement - especially for adolescents
  5. Relapse prevention - ongoing monitoring
