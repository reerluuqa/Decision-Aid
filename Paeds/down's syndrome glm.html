Delivering the Diagnosis
üí¨

Prenatal

  • Deliver in person, both parents present.
  • Explain screening is a "risk assessment," not binary.
  • Provide balanced info & resources.
  • Offer support group contact.

Postnatal

  • Inform immediately of suspicion.
  • Privately, with infant present.
  • Congratulate on birth first.
  • Discuss positive aspects + balanced medical outlook.
