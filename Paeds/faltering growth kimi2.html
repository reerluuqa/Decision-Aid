Nutritional Support

Infants: Lactation consult, increase caloric density
Children: Calorie-dense foods (avocado, peanut butter)
Supplements: After meals, not as meal replacements

Feeding Strategies

Responsive feeding: Recognize hunger/satiety cues
Consistent routines: Regular times and places
Child autonomy: Involve in meal prep, self-feeding
