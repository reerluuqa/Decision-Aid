Parameter Value
Prevalence 124 per 100,000 (lifetime)

Oxygen Therapy
100% oxygen at 12-15 L/min for 15-20 minutes via non-rebreather mask
Contraindications: Fire hazard areas, severe COPD
Sumatriptan SC
6mg subcutaneous injection. May repeat after 1 hour if headache recurs. Max 12mg/day
Contraindications: IHD, uncontrolled hypertension, MAOIs within 2 weeks
Sumatriptan Nasal
10-20mg intranasal. May repeat after 2 hours. Max 40mg/day
Age 18-65 years only
Zolmitriptan Nasal
5mg intranasal. May repeat after 2 hours. Max 10mg/day
Off-label use
