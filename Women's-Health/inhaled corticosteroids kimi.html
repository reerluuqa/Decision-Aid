ADVERSE EFFECTS - Watch For

Local Effects (Common)
  • Oral candidiasis (5-10%)
  • Dysphonia (hoarseness)
  • Throat irritation
  • Paradoxical bronchospasm
Systemic Effects (Dose Dependent)
  • Adrenal suppression
  • Growth retardation (children)
  • Osteoporosis
  • Skin thinning/easy bruising
  • Mood changes
