üìã Method-Specific Instructions

Male Condom

  • Use a new condom for each act of intercourse
  • Check expiry date and BSI/CE marking
  • Never use oil-based lubricants with latex or polyisoprene condoms (damages material)
  • Roll down to base of erect penis before genital contact
  • Withdraw before penis softens to prevent spillage
  • Dispose in bin (not toilet)

Female Condom

  • Insert before genital contact
  • Guide penis into condom (not alongside)
  • Compatible with oil-based lubricants
  • Twist outer ring and pull out after intercourse
  • Dispose in bin (not toilet)

Diaphragm/Cap

  • Must be fitted by trained clinician - use backup method until fit/skill confirmed
  • Spermicide application: Diaphragm - 2 strips on dome + rim; Cap - 1/3 full inside (not on rim)
  • Insert ‚â§3 hours before sex; leave ‚â•6 hours after
  • Remove: diaphragm ‚â§30 hours, cap ‚â§48 hours
  • Recheck fit after: 6 weeks postpartum/abortion, or ¬±3 kg weight change
