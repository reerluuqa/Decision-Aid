Identification (HARK)

Ask these questions in a safe environment.

  • Humiliation: "Has your partner humiliated or belittled you?"
  • Afraid: "Are you afraid of your partner?"
  • Rape: "Forced sex?"
  • Kick: "Physical hurt?"

Referral Pathways

Immediate Risk Pathway
Step 1: Immediate Protection
If patient in surgery or danger Call 999.
If patient has hearing/speech impairment: Relay 18000 or 18001.
Step 2: MARAC Referral
Contact Manchester IDVA to trigger Multi-Agency Risk Assessment Conference.
Consent not legally required for public protection, but good practice to inform unless risky.
Step 3: Safe Discharge
Do not send home if unsafe. Coordinate with Police.
Non-Immediate Risk Pathway
Step 1: Assessment
Use HARK/CARE model. Assess risk level (Standard/Medium).
Step 2: Referral Options
  • IRIS Programme: Refer to Advocate Educator at practice.
  • GM Helpline: Self-referral or professional referral.
  • IDVA: For Medium/High risk cases needing advocacy.
Step 3: Signposting
Provide End the Fear website directory.
Roles in the Process
GP

Identification, Safety Planning, Referral to IDVA/MARAC, Documentation.

IDVA

Independent Domestic Violence Adviser. Risk assessment (DASH), Safety planning, Advocacy at MARAC.

MARAC

Multi-Agency Risk Assessment Conference. Shares info to create safety plan for high-risk victims.

Commissioning Landscape

Understanding who funds and leads DVA services in Manchester.

Manchester City Council

Role: Lead commissioner.

  • Statutory "Safe Accommodation Duty".
  • Funds Refuges (e.g., Manchester Women's Aid).
  • Community Safety Partnership & Partnership Board.
  • Strategy 2024-26.
GMCA

Role: Strategic coordination.

  • Gender-Based Violence Strategy (2024-26).
  • Commissions IDVA/ISVA services regionally.
  • Funds perpetrator interventions.
  • #IsThisOK campaign.
NHS GM ICB

Role: Health & Safeguarding.

  • Place-based safeguarding teams.
  • Commissioning health services.
  • IRIS programme funding.
  • Representation on MARAC boards.
Integrated Care Partnership (ICP)

The NHS GM ICP collaborates wider than just NHS, focusing on health and wellbeing strategies. MLCO (Manchester Local Care Organisation) indicates a split: NHS GM commissions most adult community health, while MCC commissions children's 0-19 and public health. Close collaboration is essential.
