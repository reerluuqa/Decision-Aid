Management Protocol
1

First Line (All)

Coconut oil perineal massage (2× daily) + Vulval health (no soap, simple emollients)

2

Specific Targets

Atrophy → Oestrogens | Dermatosis → Steroids + Emollient | Vulvodynia → MDT

3

Physio Referral

Pelvic floor dysfunction (NG210): overactivity, prolapse, chronic pain

4

Psychosexual

Institute of Psychosexual Medicine. Facilitate understanding, don't "fix"

Aids for Intercourse

Lubricants 5% Lidocaine
Investigations: USS for masses. Ferritin if pruritis vulvae.
