Background & Guidance

UKMEC 2016 prioritises Long-Acting Reversible Contraceptives (LARCs) as first-line agents. Categories are not additive. If multiple UKMEC 3 conditions coexist, the method generally becomes UKMEC 4. This tool is a guide and does not replace clinical judgement.

Progestogen-Only (POP/Implant/Injection)

DMPA (Injection) Specifics

Age: UKMEC 2 if <18y or >45y. Review BMD every 2y.

Vascular Risk: UKMEC 3 if multiple risk factors (due to low estrogen effects on HDL).

Implant / POP Specifics

Vascular Risk: UKMEC 2 even with multiple risk factors (safer profile than injection).

Postpartum: UKMEC 1 even <6w. No effect on milk supply.
