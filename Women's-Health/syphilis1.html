Examination

  • Genital, oral, skin (palms/soles, scalp)
  • Neurological and ocular exam
  • Look for chancres, rash, condylomata lata, lymphadenopathy

  • Expert interpretation and follow-up

Explain: Avoid all sexual contact until diagnosis/treatment confirmed.
