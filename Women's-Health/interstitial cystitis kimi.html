üìãDiagnostic Criteria

AUA Guidelines (2022)

  • Pelvic pain/pressure/discomfort
  • Duration >6 weeks
  • No infection or identifiable cause
  • Urinary symptoms present

Supporting Evidence

  • Small frequent voids
  • Negative urine culture
  • Glomerulations on cystoscopy
  • Hunner's ulcers (10% cases)

‚úÖFirst-Line Treatment

Non-Pharmacological

  • Patient education
  • Dietary modifications
  • Stress management
  • Bladder training
  • Pelvic floor PT

Initial Medications

  • Pentosan polysulfate (FDA approved)
  • Antihistamines (hydroxyzine)
  • TCAs (amitriptyline)
  • Analgesics (NSAIDs)
  • Anticholinergics (if OAB symptoms)

üíâIntravesical Therapy

Options

  • DMSO (FDA approved)
  • Heparin + Lidocaine
  • Hyaluronic acid
  • Botulinum toxin A

Protocol

  • Weekly x 6-8 weeks
  • Maintenance monthly
  • 15 min retention time
  • Can self-administer
