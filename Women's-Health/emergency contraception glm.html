Drug Interactions
Drug Category Action
Enzyme Inducers Use Cu-IUD. Avoid UPA/LNG (Reduced efficacy).
Ulipristal (UPA) Delay Hormonal Contraception: Wait 5 days after UPA before starting progestogens.

*Includes: Rifampicin, Rifabutin, Antiepileptics, St John's Wort, HIV protease inhibitors.
Breastfeeding

Advantages vs Disadvantages

Copper IUD

Advantages
  • Most effective
  • Provides ongoing contraception
  • No drug interactions
  • Not affected by weight
Disadvantages
  • Invasive procedure
  • Requires trained professional
  • Pain on insertion
  • Unscheduled bleeding risk

Ulipristal (UPA)

Advantages
  • Effective up to 5 days
  • More effective than LNG if >72h
  • Oral administration
Disadvantages
  • No ongoing contraception
  • Drug interaction risk
  • Delay starting regular hormones
  • Adverse effects (mood, dizziness)

Levonorgestrel (LNG)

Advantages
  • Oral administration
  • Good safety profile
  • Readily available
Disadvantages
  • Shorter window (72h)
  • Less effective with higher weight
  • Less effective near ovulation
  • No ongoing contraception
