Immediate suspicion if:

  • Persistent unexplained abnormal vaginal bleeding
  • Intermenstrual or Postcoital bleeding
  • Unexplained persistent vaginal discharge (blood-stained)
  • Pelvic pain and/or dyspareunia
  • Postmenopausal bleeding (not on HRT or unexplained on HRT)
  • Visible ulcerating/necrotic lesion or inflamed/friable cervix

Advice: Most effective before sexual activity begins. The 9-valent vaccine (Gardasil 9) protects against warts and other HPV-related cancers.

Contraception & Lifestyle

Barrier Methods

Use condoms to reduce (but not eliminate) HPV transmission and other STIs (e.g., HIV).

Limit Partners

Reducing number of sexual partners lowers exposure risk.

Psychosocial & Physical

  • Depression, anxiety, body image issues.
  • Sexual dysfunction (dryness, dyspareunia).
  • Early menopause/infertility (surgery/radiotherapy).

Advanced Disease

  • Renal failure (ureteric obstruction).
  • Severe haemorrhage.
  • Fistulae (vesicovaginal/rectovaginal).
  • Lymphoedema (lower leg swelling).
