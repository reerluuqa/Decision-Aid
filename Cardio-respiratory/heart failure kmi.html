Drug Interactions & Contraindications

Contraindications

  • Bilateral renal artery stenosis
  • Serum K+ >5.0 mmol/L
  • Severe aortic stenosis
  • Pregnancy (2nd/3rd trimester)
  • History of ACE-I angioedema

Key Interactions

  • NSAIDs - Avoid (renal deterioration)
  • Potassium supplements - Avoid
  • Spironolactone + ACE-I - Monitor K+
  • Beta-blockers - Caution with verapamil
