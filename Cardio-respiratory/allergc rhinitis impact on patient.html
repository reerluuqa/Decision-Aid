Severe (7/10)

Cognitive & Work Impact

  • "Brain fog" - difficulty concentrating and making decisions
  • Reduced productivity at work/school
  • Frequent errors and longer task completion

Improvement Strategies

  • Targeted medication timing: Take non-sedating antihistamines before work/study
  • Environmental control: Use HEPA air purifiers in workspace
  • Breaks & hydration: Schedule regular short breaks and drink plenty of water
  • Discuss medication review: Consider leukotriene receptor antagonists or intranasal corticosteroids

Sleep & Fatigue

  • Frequent nighttime awakenings
  • Chronic unrefreshing sleep
  • Daytime fatigue and exhaustion

Improvement Strategies

  • Bedroom allergen-proofing: Allergen-proof mattress/pillow covers
  • Shower before bed: Wash pollen from hair and skin
  • Elevate head: Use extra pillows to reduce nocturnal congestion
  • Nasal irrigation: Saline rinse before bed to clear airways
  • Medication timing: Consider evening dose of intranasal corticosteroid

Physical Activities

  • Exercise intolerance and breathlessness
  • Avoidance of outdoor activities
  • Reduced fitness and endurance

Improvement Strategies

  • Time activities wisely: Exercise when pollen counts are lowest (usually late afternoon/after rain)
  • Indoor alternatives: Gym workouts or swimming on high pollen days
  • Pre-medicate: Take antihistamine 30-60 mins before exercise
  • Nasal breathing filter: Consider using a nasal filter during outdoor activities

Social & Recreational Life

  • Social withdrawal and isolation
  • Embarrassment from symptoms in public
  • Missed events and gatherings

Improvement Strategies

  • Plan ahead: Check pollen forecasts before social commitments
  • Host rather than visit: Control environment by hosting gatherings
  • Communicate openly: Explain condition to friends/family to reduce embarrassment
  • Carry a "rescue kit": Tissues, saline spray, antihistamines for symptom flare-ups

Practical & Sensory Issues

  • Raw, sore nose from frequent wiping
  • Reduced sense of smell and taste
  • Voice changes and communication difficulties

Improvement Strategies

  • Soft tissues: Use lotion-infused or aloe vera tissues
  • Barrier cream: Apply petroleum jelly around nostrils to protect skin
  • Nasal saline: Regular use to maintain nasal moisture and improve smell
  • Voice care: Stay hydrated and consider steam inhalation for voice

Emotional & Mental Health

  • Irritability and frustration
  • Anxiety about symptom flare-ups
  • Feelings of helplessness or depression

Improvement Strategies

  • Validate the experience: Acknowledge this is a real medical condition, not "just allergies"
  • Set realistic expectations: Focus on management, not complete cure
  • Stress management: Incorporate mindfulness or breathing exercises
  • Support networks: Connect with allergy support groups (online or local)
  • Consider referral: For persistent low mood or anxiety, discuss referral to counselling
