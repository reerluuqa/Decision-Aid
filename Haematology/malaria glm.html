OBSERVE / OUTPATIENT

ONLY IF:

  • Non-falciparum malaria
  • Capacity for daily monitoring exists
  • Treated by experienced clinician

Observe for at least 8 hours after starting therapy.

Diagnosis & Investigation

Required Tests (EDTA Sample)

  • Gold Standard: Thick & Thin Blood Films (Same Day Result).
  • Alternative: Antigen Detection Test.
  • Repeat if negative: 12-24 hours later, and again 24 hours after that.

Important Notes

Primary Care Testing: Only if result can be returned within 2 hours. Otherwise refer to A&E/MAU.

Pregnancy: Thick films can be negative even if parasites are in placenta. Seek expert advice urgently.
