Sickle Cell Trait

Note drug interaction with oral contraceptives. Advise additional contraception or barrier method.

üìò Background Information

Definition

Metabolic disorder with persistent hyperglycaemia (>11.1 mmol/L random) caused by insulin resistance and relative insulin deficiency.

Note: 'Type 2' has replaced 'Non-insulin dependent diabetes (NIDDM)' and 'Adult-onset' as insulin may be required.

Classification
  • Type 1: Absolute insulin deficiency.
  • Type 2: Insulin resistance + relative deficiency.
  • Gestational: Hyperglycaemia in pregnancy.
  • Other: Monogenic (MODY), Secondary (Pancreatitis), Endocrine (Cushing's), Drug-induced.
