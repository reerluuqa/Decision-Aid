üß© Comorbidity Assessment Screen Early

Identify and manage comorbidities immediately - do not wait for weight loss:

  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Hypertension
  • Dyslipidaemia
  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease
  • Sleep apnoea
  • Musculoskeletal conditions

üíä Treatment

üéØ Behavioural Interventions First Line

  • Discuss concerns, barriers, and personal circumstances
  • Agree realistic, personalized health goals
  • Emphasize sustainable dietary changes and physical activity
  • Review progress regularly
  • Involve family/support networks with permission

ü•ó Dietary Advice

Create energy deficit through:

  • Individualized approach considering preferences and comorbidities
  • Nutritionally balanced, long-term sustainable diet
  • Avoid restrictive, unbalanced diets
