Immediate Management

1. Alpha-blockade FIRST
Terazosin 1mg daily or Phenoxybenzamine 10mg BID
2. Volume expansion
Hydration + high-salt diet (>5g/day) for 7-14 days
3. Beta-blockade AFTER alpha-block
Atenolol 25-100mg daily (only after adequate alpha-block)
4. Surgical referral
Laparoscopic adrenalectomy when stable
