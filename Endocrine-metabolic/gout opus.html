üîç When to Suspect Gout
Classic Presentation: Rapid onset (often overnight) of severe pain, redness, swelling in 1st MTP joint
  • 1st MTP joint - affected in 50% initial attacks, 70% at some point
  • Midfoot, ankle, knee, fingers, wrist, elbow
  • Maximum intensity within 24 hours
  • Usually monoarticular
  • Tophi - firm white nodules (suggests longstanding disease)
