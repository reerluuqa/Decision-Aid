Drug Monitoring
Carbimazole: Risk of agranulocytosis. Patient must report fever, sore throat, mouth ulcers immediately.
Propylthiouracil: Risk of severe liver injury. Monitor LFTs if symptoms of hepatitis develop.
Drug Initial Monitoring Ongoing
Carbimazole FBC, LFTs before start TFTs every 6 weeks
Propylthiouracil FBC, LFTs before start TFTs every 6 weeks
