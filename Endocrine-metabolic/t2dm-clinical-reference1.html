Euglycaemic DKA Risk with Gliflozins

Patients on SGLT2 inhibitors can develop DKA with normal or mildly elevated glucose. Always consider this in unwell patients taking gliflozins. SICK DAY RULES APPLY!

üÜò

When to Stop Gliflozins IMMEDIATELY

  • ‚óè Acute illness (fever, vomiting, D&V)
  • ‚óè Risk of dehydration
  • ‚óè Peri-operative periods
  • ‚óè Reduced oral intake
  • ‚óè Previous DKA history
  • ‚óè Very low carbohydrate/ketogenic diets

Symptomatic Hyperglycaemia

Consider rescue therapy with insulin or sulphonylurea if:

  • ‚óè Polyuria, polydipsia, weight loss
  • ‚óè Blurred vision
  • ‚óè Fatigue, lethargy
  • ‚óè Recurrent infections

‚ö†Ô∏è Renal Impairment Warning

Many drugs are restricted even in mild renal impairment. Always check BNF/SPC for eGFR requirements before prescribing. Metformin requires dose reduction at eGFR <45 and contraindicated at eGFR <30.

üí° Remember: "Met's Mates" vs "Use Because You Choose"

When intensifying treatment, use one of the "Met's mates" (gliclazide, gliflozin, gliptin, pioglitazone) UNLESS there is a good reason to pick a "use because you choose" drug (e.g., insulin, GLP-1) based on specific patient factors.

üèÉ Lifestyle Interventions

üí™
Lifestyle is First-Line Therapy

Lifestyle change has benefits across ALL aspects of diabetes management. Discuss at EVERY consultation. Any sensible diet resulting in energy deficit is beneficial.

ü•ó

Nutrition

  • ‚úì Mediterranean diet (best evidence)
  • ‚úì Low-carb diets (good glycaemic control)
  • ‚úì Time-restricted fasting (5:2, 18:6)
  • ! Any diet causing energy deficit

Focus on sustainable, enjoyable habits rather than restriction

‚ö°

Activity

  • ‚úì Any increase is beneficial
  • ‚úì Post-prandial exercise best for glycaemic control
  • ‚úì 45+ mins for max benefit
  • ‚úì Include strength training

500 extra steps/day = 2-9% reduction in CV mortality

üò¥

Sleep

  • ! Poor sleep worsens glycaemic control
  • ! 50% of T2DM patients have sleep apnoea
  • ! Quantity matters (7-8 hours optimal)
  • ! Irregular sleep patterns harmful

Ask about sleep quality specifically

Weight Loss

  • ‚úì 5-15% progressive benefits
  • ‚úì ‚â•10% for CV/mortality benefits
  • ‚úì Pharmacological: Orlistat, Liraglutide, Semaglutide
  • ‚úì Surgery: ~40% achieve remission
