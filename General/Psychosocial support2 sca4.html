Acute / Red-Flag Presentations
SAME DAY Emergency referrals for bleeding, chest pain, neuro deficits, suspected cancer
Triggers: Bleeding in pregnancy, chest pain, neuro symptoms, weight loss + anaemia, suspected malignancy, jaundice, PR bleed, haemoptysis, syncope

Expected Services:

  • A&E / Acute Medical Unit - For immediate life-threatening presentations
  • 2-Week-Wait Cancer Pathway - For suspected malignancy features
  • EPAU (Early Pregnancy Assessment Unit) - For bleeding in pregnancy
  • Specialty Referrals: Neurology, Haematology, Gastroenterology, Urology
  • Radiology: Urgent USS, CT, MRI via acute pathways

Safeguarding & Domestic Abuse
SAME DAY Protective and supportive, not punitive
Triggers: Domestic violence, safeguarding child/adult, FGM risk, coercive relationships, missed appointments (DNA)

Expected Services:

  • Safeguarding Team (Children/Adults) - Mandatory referral for abuse/neglect
  • Social Services - For family support and intervention
  • MARAC (Multi-Agency Risk Assessment Conference) - For high-risk domestic abuse
  • IDVA (Independent Domestic Violence Advisor) - Safety planning and advocacy
  • Police - ONLY if immediate danger
  • Women's Aid / Men's Aid - Shelter and support services

‚≠ê Exam Phrase:

"Safeguarding here is supportive and protective, not punitive."

"My role is to make sure everyone is safe and supported."

üí¨ Patient Communication:

Children's Services' primary goal is to work with families to build a safety net - not to remove children. I want to support you through this process."
