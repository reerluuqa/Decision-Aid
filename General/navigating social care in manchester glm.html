The Process
  • Assessment: Social Worker/Social Care Assessor visits home.
  • Criteria: Care Act 2014 eligibility (impact on wellbeing).
  • Planning: Strengths-based approach + Personal Budget calculation.
  • Financials: Council pays if savings < ¬£23,250.
