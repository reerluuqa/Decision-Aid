Information Sharing

High-risk cases (e.g., MARAC):

You can share information with other agencies without consent if there is a serious risk of harm to the patient or their children ('vital interests' or 'public interest').

However, always inform the patient unless doing so increases risk.

Silent Solution Protocol

If the patient is unable to speak:

  1. Call 999.
  2. Listen to the operator's questions.
  3. Respond by pressing 55 on the keypad when prompted.

This alerts the police that the call is genuine and requires help, without the patient having to speak.
